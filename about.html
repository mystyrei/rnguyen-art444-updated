About Me

Raena Nguyen was born and raised in San Diego, California. She is a third year student at SDSU majoring in Multimedia. She is passionate about everything from 3D modeling and fabrication to graphic design. She wants to become a multifaceted designer with a diverse skillset.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will you be graduating from SDSU?

I will be graduating May 2027.

What do you have planned for after graduation?

I hopefully will get a job. Ideally I would be using the skills I learned in college, if not I’ll be working minimum wage at a cafe or something in the meantime while I try to find something.

What was your favorite class you took at SDSU?

That’s honestly a hard choice. I have a handful of classes that I thoroughly enjoyed but I would have to choose between my Black and White Film photography class and Woodworking class. Both were really fun and I liked being hands-on with developing film and making things.

What work are you most proud producing at SDSU?

So far, I would say that it’s the brochure I made during my sophomore year. I was able to take creative liberties with where I took the project and I’m happy that I got to have fun designing it.
